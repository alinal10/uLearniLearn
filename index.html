uLearniLearn

A pomodoro-style timer that learns what time intervals help YOU learn best.

Timer

Directions: Click "Set Work" to set how many minutes you want to work for. Next, click "Start" to begin your timer, you can click again to pause. Once your time is over, click "Set Break" to set how many minutes you want to have a break for. Click "Start" again. Once your break ends, your cycle is over. Rate that cycle using buttons below the timer. End your study session or begin a new cycle by repeating these steps. Beside recommended intervals show what intervals we estimate are best for you.

Analytics

Though your recommended intervals are already calculated and displayed above the timer, you can also view your analytics here by pressing "Generate Data." With numerical data shown on the right and graphical data shown on the left, you can see what intervals are amazing, good, okay, not so good, and terrible for you. To update shown data, you must click the button again. Please note that all data is shown in minutes.
